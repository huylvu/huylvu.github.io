The Tax Simulation App

A DEPOCEN internal app built to simulate tobacco-tax scenarios across the base year and the following five years. It helps researchers and policymakers compare how specific tax paths can affect cigarette consumption, retail price, tax revenue, smoking prevalence, and smoking-related deaths.

Tobacco Mapping 2025

An internal mapping app for exploring tobacco retailers in Hanoi and Ho Chi Minh City. It combines store locations, product and brand availability, prices, and retail characteristics so users can search shops and inspect the local retail environment in detail.
