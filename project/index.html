2024 Evaluating open data portals in Vietnam See More

Role: Data Analyst, Assessment Model Builder

Description: This project develops a set of indicators to evaluate open data portals in Vietnam. From there, we will develop a set of criteria to build open data portals in Vietnam. In addition, the research team also conducted an in-depth survey to assess the understanding and need to use open data of subjects in Vietnam such as: public sector, private sector, research institutes, universities. learn. We evaluated 32 open data portals and collected opinions from more than 1,000 units and individuals who responded to the survey.

2024 Role of Access to Finance in SME Development in Viet Nam See More

Role: Data Analyst

Description: This study investigates the impact of access to finance on Small and Medium Enterprise (SME) development in Vietnam, with a specific focus on evaluating the economic effects of ADB's 2021 loan to Vietnam International Bank (VIB). The research examines how lessening credit constraints affects job creation and local economic development, building on established literature that links SME financing to firms’ growth and production expansion.
